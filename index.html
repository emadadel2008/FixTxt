FixTxt

Fix your Right-to-Left (RTL) text when you mix it with Left-to-Right (LTR) text — instantly, right in your browser.

What is FixTxt?

FixTxt is a web tool that corrects the text alignment in the proper flow of the language. Some apps do not support RTL languages like Arabic — when you mix LTR characters into Arabic text, words get misaligned and hard to read. FixTxt wraps each line with the correct Unicode directional markers to fix the display instantly.

How to use FixTxt?

Paste or type your text

Click the RTL or Auto-detect button

Copy the result and use it anywhere
